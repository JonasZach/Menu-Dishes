Roasted Butternut Squash Risotto

Description: Creamy Arborio rice slow-cooked with vegetable broth, roasted butternut squash, garlic, and fresh thyme. Finished with vegan butter and toasted pumpkin seeds for a nutty crunch.

Dietary Information:

  • Vegan
  • Gluten-Free
  • Contains Nuts
  • Dairy-Free
  • Vegetarian

Ingredients: Arborio rice, butternut squash, garlic, onion, olive oil, vegetable broth, nutritional yeast, vegan butter, thyme, black pepper, sea salt, pumpkin seeds.
